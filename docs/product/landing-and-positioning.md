# Landing and Positioning

## Landing objective

Convert interested collectors into waitlist signups.

## Primary CTA

- Waitlist signup: "Leave your email and we will notify you at launch."

## Messaging goals

Users should quickly feel:

- "This is exactly my problem"
- "This product is built for my buying habits"
- "I want early access"

## Recommended section order

1. Hero with immediate waitlist form
2. User recognition (pain points)
3. Value-focused feature blocks
4. FAQs
5. Final CTA

## Messaging themes

- Purchase chaos across chats/sites
- Uncertainty around shipment progress
- Surprise pre-order balances
- Confidence from store trust signals and clear tracking
