# Vision and Problem

## Product overview

PandaTrack is a web app that helps collectors track stores, purchases, pre-orders, payments, and shipments in one place.

## Problem statement

Collectors often buy from multiple channels (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, websites, email invoices). Order details become fragmented, which causes:

- Lost purchase history
- Duplicate buying risk
- Unclear shipment status
- Unexpected pre-order balances and budgeting stress

PandaTrack centralizes this information so collecting stays exciting instead of chaotic.

## Target users

PandaTrack is built for collectors who:

- Buy from multiple stores/channels
- Manage several active orders at once
- Need visibility into shipments and remaining balances
- Want a reliable history and fewer surprises

## Core value proposition

- One source of truth for purchases and shipments
- Clear status visibility at a glance
- Better payment planning and budget confidence
- Lower buying risk through store trust signals

## Product principles

- Fast and simple interactions
- Scannable, decision-ready UI
- Centralized and reliable data
- Collector-first workflows (pre-orders, long waits, split shipments)
- Trust and clarity in every screen
